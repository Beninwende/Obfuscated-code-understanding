Exercise: Find the Next Prime Number Problem Statement Your task is to find the smallest prime number that is greater than or equal to a given integer X. A prime number is a positive integer greater than 1 that cannot be exactly divided by any integer other than 1 and itself. For example, the numbers 2, 3, 5, and 7 are prime numbers, while 4, 6, and 8 are not. Write a program that takes an integer X and outputs the smallest prime number that is greater than or equal to X.Requirements
