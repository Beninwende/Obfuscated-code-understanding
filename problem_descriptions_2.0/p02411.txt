Exercise: Student Grading System Problem Statement: Write a program that evaluates the performance of students based on their test scores. Each student has three scores: m: the midterm score (out of 50) f: the final exam score (out of 50) r: the makeup exam score (out of 100) A score of -1 indicates that the student did not take the corresponding exam. The program should calculate the student's grade based on the following rules: If the student did not take the midterm or final exam (i.e., any of m or f is -1), the grade is F. If the total score of m + f is greater than or equal to 80, the grade is A. If the total score of m + f is greater than or equal to 65 but less than 80, the grade is B. If the total score of m + f is greater than or equal to 50 but less than 65, the grade is C. If the total score of m + f is greater than or equal to 30 but less than 50, the grade is D. However, if the makeup exam score r is greater than or equal to 50, the grade is C. If the total score of m + f is less than 30, the grade is F.
