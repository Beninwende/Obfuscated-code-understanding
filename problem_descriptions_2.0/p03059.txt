**Title: Calculate Total Weight of Packages Delivered**

**Problem Statement:**

You are working with a delivery company that uses a system to track the total weight of packages delivered. Every package has a specific weight, and deliveries are made at specific regular intervals. Your task is to determine the total weight of all packages delivered within a certain period after the delivery process begins.

The delivery system processes packages as follows:
1. A package of weight `W` is delivered at regular intervals of `I` minutes after the delivery process is activated.
2. The delivery happens at I minutes, 2I minutes, 3I minutes, and so on.
3. Find the total weight of all packages delivered within the first `D` minutes after the delivery process starts.

**Requirements:**
- Your function should calculate the sum of the weights of all packages delivered within the specified `D` minutes period.
- Consider deliveries that happen exactly at the `D` minute mark.
- Input values are positive integers.
