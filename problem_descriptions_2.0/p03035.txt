**Exercise Title: Determine Ticket Price Based on Age**

**Problem Statement:**

Create a function that calculates the ticket price based on the age of a visitor. Different age groups have different pricing rules which determine how much they need to pay for a standard entry ticket.

You are required to implement a function `calculate_ticket_price` that takes two parameters:
1. `age` (integer): The age of the visitor.
2. `base_price` (integer): The standard price of the ticket.

The pricing rules are as follows:
- Individuals aged 0 to 4 years are admitted free of charge.
- Individuals aged 5 to 12 years pay half the base price.
- Individuals aged 13 years and older pay the full base price.

**Requirements:**
- Implement the function `calculate_ticket_price(age, base_price)`.
- Return the ticket price as an integer based on the given rules.
- Ensure your function can handle any valid input within reasonable constraints.
- Consider edge cases such as minimum and maximum age values.

**Constraints:**
- \(0 \leq \text{age} \leq 120\)
- \(10 \leq \text{base\_price} \leq 200\)
- The `base_price` is an integer.

Your task is to compute and return the ticket price according to these rules, given the `age` and `base_price` of the visitor. Focus on handling the conditions and returning the correct amount.
