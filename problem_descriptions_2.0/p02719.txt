**Title: Calculate Minimum Difference**

**Problem Statement:**

You are given two integers, `A` and `B`. The goal is to find the smallest possible positive difference between `A` and any multiple of `B`. To achieve this, you may repeatedly perform the following operation any number of times:

- Calculate the absolute difference between `A` and the closest multiple of `B`.

Keep applying the operation until the smallest possible result is achieved. 

**Requirements:**

1. Implement a function that accepts two integers, `A` and `B`.
2. The output should be the smallest positive difference between `A` and any multiple of `B`.
3. Ensure the solution is efficient and can handle large values of `A` and `B`.
4. Consider that both `A` and `B` are guaranteed to be non-negative and within the range of [0, 10^18].

Your task is to create a function that calculates and returns this minimum difference.
